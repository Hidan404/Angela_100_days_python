Angela`Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cup war milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated suga
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

Instructions

  1. Warm the milk and dissolve the yeast in it. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix the sugar, egg, egg yolk, and melted butter in a large bowl.
  3. Add the yeast mixture to the bowl and stir until combined.
  4. Gradually add the bread flour and knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  5. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours.
  6. Roll out the dough, spread the filling, and roll it into a log.
  7. Cut the log into rolls, place them in a baking dish, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.
